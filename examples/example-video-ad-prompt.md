# Example: Video Ad Prompt

```text
Use the uploaded Video Prompting kit.

Project:
Launch ad for an internal AI assistant that helps teams turn research into reusable deliverables.

Audience:
Product, design, and operations teams who have too much scattered work and not enough reusable process.

Goal:
Make viewers understand that the assistant turns messy input into structured outputs quickly.

Channel:
LinkedIn and internal Teams announcement.

Length:
30 seconds.

Aspect ratio:
16:9 first, with a possible 1:1 cutdown.

Brand:
Use approved logo and existing brand colors. Keep tone confident, practical, and creative.

Assets:
Logo, product screenshots, three workflow screenshots, approved CTA.

CTA:
Try the assistant on your next project brief.

Please:
1. Ask missing questions.
2. Recommend format.
3. Create a storyboard.
4. Then write shot-by-shot prompts for an AI video tool.
5. Include negative prompts and QA checklist.
```

## Expected Structure

- Hook: scattered files and messy requests
- Shift: assistant organizes work
- Proof: product UI and examples
- Outcome: reusable deliverables
- CTA: try it on next brief

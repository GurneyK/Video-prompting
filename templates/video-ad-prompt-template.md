```text
[audience]

[goal]

[platform, aspect ratio, duration, sound/caption needs]

Brand:
[colors, tone, typography, logo rules, legal lines]

Assets available:
[logos, screenshots, UI, product shots, approved copy]

Before creating final prompts, do this:
1. Ask missing questions.
2. Recommend the best format.
3. Create a storyboard with scene timing, visual, motion, copy, audio, assets, and QA risks.
4. Write final shot-by-shot prompts for an AI video tool.
5. Include negative prompts and production notes.
```

## Ad Story Pattern

Use this default structure unless the project needs something else:

1. Hook: problem, ambition, or surprising visual
2. Tension: why the current way is painful
3. Product/idea reveal
4. Proof or concrete example
5. Transformation
6. CTA

## QA

- Works with sound off
- First 2 seconds are clear
- Text is readable on mobile
- CTA appears in final 20 percent
- Brand and logo are approved
- No unlicensed music, footage, or customer marks

# Example: Showreel Prompt

```text
Use the uploaded Video Prompting kit.

I need a 12-second showreel segment for a company reel.

Project:
An internal design library and AI product toolkit.

Goal:
Make the work feel polished, creative, and useful.

Assets:
Design library website link, logo, screenshots, component list, previous presentation template.

Placement:
Middle of the reel, after an intro montage and before a product demo segment.

Please:
1. Create a 5-shot storyboard.
2. Suggest transition in and transition out.
3. Identify the strongest proof visuals.
4. Include copy, motion, and sound notes.
5. Keep it readable without narration.
```

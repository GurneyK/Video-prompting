# What To Upload To AI

Upload the fewest files that give the AI enough context.

## For Any Project

Upload:

1. `AI_UPLOAD_PACKET.md`
2. One relevant template from `templates/`
3. Brand/logo rules if available
4. Product notes, screenshots, or source copy
5. Any examples you want it to match

## For A Video Ad

Upload:

- `templates/video-ad-prompt-template.md`
- product/campaign brief
- audience notes
- brand guidelines
- platform specs
- examples of ads you like or dislike

## For Animated Presentations

Upload:

- `templates/presentation-motion-template.md`
- existing deck, if any
- brand/style guide
- audience and meeting context
- required talking points

## For Product Demos

Upload:

- `templates/product-demo-template.md`
- screenshots or mockups
- product flow notes
- feature list
- target user
- success criteria

## For HTML Video Teasers

Upload:

- `templates/html-video-teaser-template.md`
- `assets/animations.jsx`
- `assets/scene-template.jsx`
- `assets/build.mjs` for Mode B
- relevant references from `references/html-video-teasers/`
- logos or SVGs approved for use

## Upload Prompt

```text
I uploaded the Video Prompting kit and the relevant source files. First, summarize what context you have. Then ask what is missing. Do not generate final output until we agree on the storyboard.
```

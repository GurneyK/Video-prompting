# Sound Design

Sound can make motion feel more premium, but it should support the structure.

## When To Add Sound

Add sound when:

- the video will be presented live
- the user will intentionally press play
- the sound supports transitions, clicks, reveals, or completion moments

Avoid autoplay sound on websites and embeds.

## Sound Layers

- Background music: mood and energy
- Whoosh: scene transition or major movement
- Click: cursor or UI action
- Chime: completion or success
- Bass hit: major reveal

## Rules

- Keep music under dialogue or SFX.
- Add sound to structural moments, not every movement.
- Always support silent playback with captions or on-screen copy.
- In web embeds, default to muted unless the user opts in.
- Confirm music/SFX licensing before external use.

## Prompt

```text
Create a sound plan for this storyboard. Include where to use music, whooshes, clicks, chimes, silence, and captions. Also identify where sound should be disabled or optional.
```
